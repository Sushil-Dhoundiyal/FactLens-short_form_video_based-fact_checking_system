Key Indian Policies Against Misinformation

IT (Intermediary Guidelines & Digital Media Ethics Code) Rules, 2021

Requires social media platforms to remove content flagged by authorities within 36 hours, establish grievance redressal mechanisms, and appoint compliance officers based in India.
Learn More →

Press Council of India Guidelines

Ethical guidelines for print and digital media journalists; includes advisories on verification processes and reporting standards to combat fake news.
Learn More →

Indian Penal Code Sections

Sections 153A, 295A, 499, and 500 applied to misinformation that promotes enmity, defames individuals, or disturbs public tranquility.
Learn More →

Fact-Check Unit of Press Information Bureau

Government unit dedicated to identifying and debunking misinformation related to government policies; citizens can report suspicious content for verification.
